About Mustang

Mustang is a remote region in the Himalayas of Nepal. It is renowned for its breathtaking landscapes, ancient monasteries, and unique Tibetan Buddhist culture. With its arid valleys, high mountains, and vibrant festivals, Mustang offers an unforgettable adventure for travelers.
